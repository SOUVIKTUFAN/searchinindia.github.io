Indian Institute of Technology,Madras

Indian Institute of Technology Madras is a public engineering institute located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. As one of the Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs), it is recognised as an Institute of National Importance.[2] Founded in 1959 with technical and financial assistance from the former government of West Germany, it was the third IIT that was established by the Government of India..
