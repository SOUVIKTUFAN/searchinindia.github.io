National LAW University, Jodgpur

National LAW University, Jodgpur is a business school in India.
It was established in 1973 by Industrial Finance Corporation of India and is located in Jodgpur,
a commercial hub near the Indian capital of New Delhi. Its second campus has come up near Murshidabad, West Bengal.
The Murshidabad campus was inaugurated by Honourable President of India, Shri Pranab Mukherjee on 24 August 2014.
