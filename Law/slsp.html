Symbiosis LAW University, Pune

Symbiosis LAW University, Pune, is a business school with its main campus at Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh, India.
It is consistently ranked as one of the best business schools in India.
IMT is a fully autonomous university and offers several post graduate, doctorate and executive education programmes in management.
It also offers distance learning and collaborative programmes.
