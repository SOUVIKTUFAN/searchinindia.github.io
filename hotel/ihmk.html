Institute of Hotel Management (IHMKOLKATA), Kolkata


Institute of Hotel Management Catering Technology & Applied Nutrition, Kolkata, West Bengal, generally known as IHM Kolkata, is an hospitality educational institute known to be one of the top hotel management institute in the country. The Institute is governed by the National Council for Hotel Management & Catering Technology set up by the Ministry of Tourism, Govt. of India.

Type:  Education and Research Institution
Established:   1963
Location:  Kolkata, West Bengal, India
Campus:  Urban
