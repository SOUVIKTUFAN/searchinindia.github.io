Kohinoor-IMI School of Hospitality Management (KIMI)


Kohinoor-IMI School of Hospitality Management is an institution of hotel and tourism management in Khandala, Maharashtra. It was established in 2000 and founded by former Chief Minister of Maharashtra and Speaker of Lok Sabha Manohar Joshi.
