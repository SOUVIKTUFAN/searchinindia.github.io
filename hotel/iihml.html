Institute of Hotel Management (IHMLUCKNOW), Lucknow


Institute of Hotel Management, Catering & Nutrition, Lucknow is a hotel management school located is located in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh, India. It was established in 1969, it is affiliated to National Council for Hotel Management and Catering Technology

Type:  Education and Research Institution
Established:   1969
