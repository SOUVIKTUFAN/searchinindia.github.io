Institute of Management and Technology (IMT PTU), Ghaziabad

Institute of Management Technology, Ghaziabad, is a business school with its main campus at Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh, India.
It is consistently ranked as one of the best business schools in India.
IMT is a fully autonomous university and offers several post graduate, doctorate and executive education programmes in management.
It also offers distance learning and collaborative programmes.

Motto in English:  Grooming Leadership through Innovation, Execution and Social Responsibility
Type:  Public business school
Established:   1980
Location:  Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh, India
Campus:  Residential
